Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our ERP systems, POS software, installation, customization, and support services

What is included in your ERP system?

Our comprehensive ERP system includes modules for finance management, purchase and sales processing, inventory control, reporting and analytics, and overall business operations management. All modules are fully integrated and can be customized to fit your specific business needs.

Can the POS system work offline?

Yes, our POS solution offers both online and offline modes. The offline mode does not require an internet connection and allows you to process transactions seamlessly. The online mode requires internet access and enables real-time data syncing to the cloud. You can choose the mode that best fits your business operations.

How long does installation and setup take?

Installation time varies based on your business size and requirements. Typically, basic setup takes 1-2 weeks, including system installation, data migration, and initial training. For complex customizations, the timeline may extend to 4-6 weeks. We provide a detailed project timeline during consultation.

What kind of support do you provide after installation?

We offer comprehensive support including email support, phone assistance, remote troubleshooting, and on-site visits when needed. Support levels vary by plan - from standard business hours support to 24/7 priority support for enterprise clients. We also provide regular system updates and maintenance.

How secure is my data with cloud-based storage?

We implement enterprise-grade security measures including encrypted data transmission, secure cloud storage with daily automated backups, access controls, and regular security audits. Your data is protected with industry-standard security protocols, and we offer both cloud-based and on-premise deployment options based on your security requirements.
